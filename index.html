Baby Sleep Calendar & Log

Welcome to Baby Sleep Calendar & Log, the intuitive app crafted for contemporary parents. This app helps you track and optimize your child's sleep patterns effectively.

Getting Started

The app presents a monthly view of your baby’s sleep log, allowing you to input new sleep data easily and review detailed information for each day.

Logging Sleep Data

  • Quality of Sleep: Good Sleep, Fair Sleep, Poor Sleep.
  • Night Awakenings: Record the number of times your baby woke up during the night.
  • Awake Time During the Night: Note the total duration your baby was awake during the night.
  • Woke Up: Enter the time your baby woke up in the morning.
  • Night Sleep Hours: Automatically calculated based on the 'fell asleep' time.
  • Number of Naps: Input the count of your baby’s naps throughout the day.
  • Day Sleep Hours: Log the cumulative amount of daytime sleep.
  • Fell Asleep: Record when your baby fell asleep in the evening.
  • Notes Section: Add any pertinent notes that could affect sleep patterns.

Editing Entries

All data entries are editable. You can revisit and modify any logged information as needed.

Monthly Calendar Overview

Once logged, entries will populate the monthly calendar view. Tap on a date to see detailed information about the sleep data recorded for that particular day.

Understanding Sleep Patterns

By consistently logging sleep information, you can track your child’s progress, identify patterns, and address potential issues, making it a companion in your parenting journey.
